Description

"Blue" is a song by Yung Kai that delves into the nuances of young love and longing. The track is characterized by its dreamy atmosphere, with lyrics that paint vivid images of affection and desire. Yung Kai employs metaphors related to nature—such as "waves," "moonlight skies," and "ocean's colors"—to express the depth and fluidity of his emotions. The chorus, "Will you be forever with me?" encapsulates the yearning for a lasting connection.
