Description

"Lily of the Valley" is a song by Korean artist Daniel, released in 2020. The track gained significant attention after being featured in the soundtrack of the Korean drama Melancholia in 2021. ​

The song's lyrics convey a message of eternal love and deep appreciation for moments spent with a partner. It uses the metaphor of a flower to symbolize tenderness and beauty in a relationship. ​
