Description

​"Dependent" is a vulnerable and emotional song that explores the aftermath of a breakup, where letting go feels nearly impossible. It captures the internal tug-of-war between knowing someone isn't right for you and still feeling anchored to the comfort of what once was. The lyrics reflect that painful in-between space—where you want to move on, but your heart hasn’t caught up.

The melody is soft and aching, echoing the sense of emotional dependence and lingering attachment. It’s the kind of song that feels like a quiet confession, pulling you into the raw, honest weight of not being ready to let someone go, even when you know you should.
