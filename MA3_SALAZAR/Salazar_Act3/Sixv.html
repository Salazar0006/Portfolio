Description

​"About You" is a poignant ballad that delves into themes of lingering memories and unresolved emotions associated with past relationships. The lyrics convey a deep sense of nostalgia and introspection, with the recurring line, "Do you think I have forgotten about you?" emphasizing the persistent thoughts of a former lover. This reflective narrative is further enriched by the inclusion of a female perspective in the bridge, adding depth to the song's exploration of mutual reminiscence and longing.

​

Musically, the track is characterized by its gothic and orchestral elements, creating an atmospheric soundscape that evokes a dreamlike quality. The production draws inspiration from the "wall of sound" technique, enveloping listeners in layers of instrumentation that mirror the complexity of revisiting past emotions. This sonic arrangement effectively complements the song's introspective lyrics, offering an immersive experience that resonates with the universal theme of reflecting on past relationships. ​
